Residents in multiple areas near downtown Princeton, including Yorkshire, Longneck, and College, reported unusual vehicle activity late last night into early morning.

Multiple witnesses say vehicles were speeding through the area repeatedly.

A white SUV and white sedan were seen driving back and forth, with the same vehicles reportedly turning around and continuing through Longneck.

A sports car (Mustang) was also seen moving through the area, at times slowly and later speeding through.

Residents describe the driving behavior as reckless, with vehicles repeatedly speeding through residential streets late at night.

This comes less than 24 hours after the earlier major police response in Princeton, which drew significant attention across the city.

This information is based on multiple witness reports. No official details have been released regarding the nature of this activity, and it is unclear whether it is connected to any prior incidents.

Residents across multiple neighborhoods noticed reckless and suspicious vehicle activity late at night, raising safety concerns in the community.

If you have any information, footage, or saw these vehicles, share it. The goal is to better understand what happened and help prevent unsafe driving in these areas.
