## EARLIER INCIDENT (E. PRINCETON DR. & N. 4TH)

A large police response was reported earlier near the 400 block of E. Princeton Dr. toward Caldwell Street.

• Multiple police units, EMS, and a helicopter responded

• Roads were blocked, and crime scene tape was reported

• Nearby schools were placed on “secure” status (not lockdown), which has since been lifted

From dispatch audio and circulating reports:

• A possible shooting was reported involving a blue pickup and a black passenger car

• One individual was reportedly shot in the leg (unconfirmed)

• The vehicle later ended up near Smitty’s Car Wash

Additional clarification:

• Witnesses reported heavy activity near the car wash

• A statement circulating from the car wash indicates the shooting did NOT occur there, but that those involved may have gone there for help

## OFFICIAL UPDATE FROM POLICE

According to Princeton Police:

• An arrest has now been made in connection with the incident

• Officers were actively working the area throughout the afternoon

• Schools have returned to normal operations

• A full media release is expected after the investigation

## LATER ACTIVITY (HWY 380 / WALMART AREA)

A second major police presence was reported later near Walmart along Highway 380.

• Traffic slowed and multiple units were seen in the parking lot

• Witnesses reported officers with guns drawn during the response

• Witnesses at the scene reported a female may have been detained and a child removed from a vehicle (unconfirmed)

• One witness described individuals in the vehicle as appearing disoriented, with one person reportedly struggling to walk (unconfirmed)

At the same time:

• Entrances and movement in the area appeared restricted, with traffic flow and access limited during the response

• Police presence remained heavy for a period before units began clearing
