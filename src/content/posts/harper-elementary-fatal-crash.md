## Summary

A fatal single-vehicle collision occurred outside Harper Elementary School on Tuesday afternoon. The crash involved a 2021 Tesla that struck seven parked vehicles. The driver, a 51 year old man from Fairview, died at the scene. No students or faculty were injured.

---

## What Happened

According to the Princeton Police Department, officers responded at about **1:45 PM on November 18** to reports of a vehicle crash in the **8000 block of County Road 398 outside Harper Elementary**.

Bystanders were already attempting to assist the driver when officers arrived. The man, who has not yet been publicly identified, was pronounced dead at the scene. Police reports state the driver lost control of the vehicle prior to impact.

---

## Impact to School Grounds

The collision struck **seven parked and unoccupied cars**. Debris from the crash reached the school building and shattered a **cafeteria window**.

Two individuals inside the cafeteria were evaluated as a precaution. Both were cleared with **no injuries**.

No students or staff were near the parked vehicles when the crash occurred. No additional injuries were reported.

---

## Vehicle and Driver Information

The driver was operating a **2021 Tesla**. Police state he was a **51 year old resident of Fairview**.

His identity has not been publicly released while **next-of-kin notification is underway**.

---

## Investigation Status

The **Princeton Police Department** has stated that the incident remains **under investigation**. No official cause has been released.

Anyone with information relevant to the investigation is encouraged to contact the Princeton Police Department at:

**972-736-3901**

---

## Community Context

The crash occurred **during school hours**. Despite the severity of the collision and the proximity to the campus, **no students were harmed**.

The school facility sustained **limited structural damage**, primarily a shattered cafeteria window.

---

## What Residents Should Know

• There is **no indication of additional public safety threats**  
• **No students or school personnel were injured**  
• Authorities are **still determining the cause**  
• The district and police have **not announced safety changes or roadway adjustments**
